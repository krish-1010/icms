About the College

Loyola College, a Catholic Minority Institution, was founded by the Society of Jesus (Jesuits) in 1925. UGC conferred the status of “College with potential for Excellence” on Loyola College in 2004 and confirmed the same in 2010. Loyola college is bestowed with an A++ grade accredited by the NAAC in the fourth cycle, on January 2021.

About the Department

The Department of Physics in Loyola College was established in 1926. The Department is currently marching forward with full enthusiasm and vigor, having produced 150 doctorates as of now.

About the conference

This Material Science Conference offers innovative ideas for researchers in the chosen field.This conference gathers various research ideas from young scholars working all over India. Meet eminent researchers and inspiring experts from the field to discuss current advances, recent innovations and implementations of materials in various domains.
